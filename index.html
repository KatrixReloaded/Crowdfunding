About muftether

Muftether is a crowdfunding project built by two broke developers, Akshat Agarwal and Kartikeya Sureka. It was developed in 2023 using Solidity and Bootstrap Studio.

Our goal is to provide a platform where users around the world can support several causes and the people in need.

We hope to satisfy both, the users and the organizations seeking our help to connect them with the people around the world to come to their aid.
